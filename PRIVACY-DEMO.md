# Demo User Privacy Statement

_Last updated: 2026-05-27_

pAIchart offers a **demo experience** so you can explore the MCP Hub platform
using your existing AI client (Claude or ChatGPT) without creating a separate
account or sharing payment details. This statement explains what a demo account
is, the small amount of data it holds, what it can and cannot do, and — most
importantly — that **demo identities are automatically deleted after 30 days of
inactivity**.

This statement covers **demo users only**. It does not cover registered
customer accounts or service owners.

---

## How a demo account is created

A demo account is created **automatically the first time you connect** to the
pAIchart MCP Hub from a supported AI client and sign in with that client's
OAuth provider:

| AI Client | OAuth Provider |
|-----------|----------------|
| Claude (Desktop / web) | GitHub |
| ChatGPT | Microsoft |

We never see or store your provider password. The sign-in happens entirely on
the provider's side; pAIchart only receives the basic profile the provider
returns after you approve the connection.

## What data we hold

A demo account stores only the **minimal profile** needed to sign you in and
show your session:

- Email address (as returned by your OAuth provider)
- Display name and avatar (if provided)
- OAuth provider name and your provider account identifier
- Timestamps for account creation and last sign-in

We do **not** collect payment information, browsing history, contacts, or any
data beyond the OAuth profile above. We do not use demo data for marketing, we
do not sell it, and we do not contact you.

## What a demo account can do

Demo accounts are intentionally limited to **read and explore**:

- **Demo resources** — read-only. You can view demonstration POVs, phases,
  tasks, and related content, but cannot create, edit, or delete them.
- **MCP services** — read and execute. You can discover registered services and
  run (execute) the approved public services exposed for demos. You **cannot**
  register, modify, or delete services.
- **Agents** — demo accounts cannot run AI agents using platform credentials. To
  run an agent you must supply your **own API key** (e.g. your own Anthropic
  key), so any such usage is billed to you, not to pAIchart.

Demo accounts are also excluded from team-member and assignee lists, so your
identity is not presented to other users as a selectable collaborator.

## Retention: automatic deletion after 30 days

**Demo identities are automatically and permanently deleted after 30 days of
inactivity**, measured from your last sign-in.

When a demo account is deleted, the user record and its associated data
(including any workflow-execution history tied to the account) are **hard-deleted
from the database** — not merely deactivated or hidden. There is no archival copy
of the demo profile retained after deletion.

If you sign in again before the 30-day window elapses, the clock resets and your
demo account continues.

## Requesting earlier deletion

If you would like your demo account and its data removed **before** the automatic
30-day window, contact us at **privacy@paichart.com** with the email address
associated with your demo session, and we will delete it.

## Changes to this statement

We may update this statement as the demo experience evolves. The "Last updated"
date above reflects the most recent change.
